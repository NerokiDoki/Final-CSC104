About Tech Time and Learn

Welcome to Tech Time and Learn, a non-profit organization focused on empowering individuals of all ages to gain the technological skills they need to thrive in today’s digital world. Our mission is to provide a community-based learning center where people from all walks of life can learn, grow, and succeed in technology.

Tech Time and Learn was born out of the need for a dedicated space where individuals can develop essential tech skills at any stage of their life. In our fast-paced, tech-driven society, it’s important to bridge the gap between those with technological expertise and those without. Our goal is to provide affordable and accessible tech education for people of all backgrounds, whether they’re looking to start a new career, improve their current skills, or simply understand the technology that surrounds them.

Unlike traditional community centers that focus on physical activities, we specialize in offering courses and hands-on experiences that cover a wide range of tech topics—from basic computer skills to more advanced areas like cybersecurity, web development, and artificial intelligence. Our flexible learning options, including weekly, monthly, and yearly subscriptions, are designed to cater to different schedules and budgets, ensuring that everyone has access to the tools they need to succeed.

At Tech Time and Learn, we also offer small group events and larger conferences that allow participants to collaborate, share knowledge, and network with others who share their passion for technology. Whether you’re an aspiring developer, an entrepreneur, or simply someone looking to learn a new skill, we’re here to help you achieve your goals.

Our organization is built on the belief that knowledge is power, and we want to provide everyone with the opportunity to learn in a welcoming, inclusive environment. Through partnerships with industry leaders like Dell and Microsoft, we ensure that our learning spaces are equipped with the latest technology and that our instructors are trained to provide the best possible education to our students.

We believe in a hands-on, interactive learning approach where students are encouraged to learn by doing, with a curriculum designed to engage and challenge them every step of the way. Tech Time and Learn is more than just a learning center; it’s a community hub for technology enthusiasts, creators, and innovators.

We’re committed to ensuring that our learning center is open to all. Regardless of age, background, or prior experience, we welcome anyone who wants to learn about technology and gain valuable skills for the future. Join us at Tech Time and Learn and be part of the next generation of tech experts!
